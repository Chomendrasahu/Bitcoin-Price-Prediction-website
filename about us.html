About Project

The purpose of this project is to present a detailed description of Bitcoin price analysis which enables the user, investor, and the companies to predict what will be for the price in the future for their better investment. The use of computational and statistical approaches in Bitcoin analytics is gaining popularity for investment comparison, visualisation, and prediction of future price. In this thesis, we will apply techniques from machine learning to the bitcoin previous price dataset to price drop/high and observe how the Adjacent close, price and volume affect the price outcomes. The main purpose of my project is to predict and show the future price of bitcoin through previous data, which make user known when to start investing and when to withdraw their money.

We are using data of bitcoin which we have taken from yfinance yahoo. To simplify the data, we have taken Adj close as our dependent value.
